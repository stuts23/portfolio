About Me

Stuti Lilani

I am overwhelmed by the impact good and intuitive designs have on humans and aim to create experiences and interactions that blur the lines between humans and computers. I have been exploring the domain since my first year and currently lead a team of 13 designers at SDSLabs, IIT Roorkee. I am well versed in all stages of the product design cycle and have experience working with industry-standard tools.

I recognize the importance of humanizing technology and creating interfaces that complement lives and add value to people using them. I enjoy seeing things from a fresh perspective
